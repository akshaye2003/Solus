OUR SERVICES

BUS SHELTERS

Bus stop adverts, impossible to ignore or switch off, serve as powerful local campaigning tools. Connecting directly with a local audience, their ground-level presence enhances effectiveness. Moreover, their cost-effectiveness allows easy alterations, tailoring relevance to various locations, maximizing impact. Embrace bus stop ads for impactful, location-specific communication, reaching your target audience effectively.

Services

BILLBOARDS

Billboards are iconic large-format outdoor advertising displays strategically placed in high-traffic areas like roads, highways, and city centers. They effectively reach a massive audience, capturing the attention of commuters, pedestrians, and travelers. With prominent positions where people live, work, and socialize, billboards create strong brand awareness and leave a lasting impression.

Services

POLE KIOSKS

Pole Kiosks are a highly impactful form of outdoor advertising, featuring small billboards installed back to back on electric poles along major city roads. Strategically placed in high-traffic areas, they ensure maximum visibility to pedestrians, motorists, and commuters. This makes them an ideal choice for businesses to engage with a diverse urban audience.  

Services

TRAFFIC SIGNAL

Traffic Signal advertising is a highly effective and innovative form of outdoor advertising that leverages the prominent locations of traffic signals to capture the attention of a captive audience. At busy intersections and traffic lights, advertisements are strategically placed on signal posts, offering a unique opportunity to reach a large and diverse audience.

UNIPOLES

Unipole billboards, positioned atop tall poles, are massive and highly impactful. With exceptional long-distance visibility, they consistently yield impressive results. The distinctive rectangular shape of unipoles offers advertisers creative freedom, enabling them to enhance message content. Harness the power of unipoles to captivate audiences from afar, ensuring your brand message stands out with brilliance and ingenuity. 

Read More

CENTER MEDIAN

Center Median ads have gained immense popularity due to their attractiveness, capturing the attention of diverse audiences. Positioned in high traffic areas within the center dividers of routes, they offer a novel approach to product promotion. With medium-sized advertisements, Center Medians effectively reach the vast number of city travelers, maximizing brand exposure.
